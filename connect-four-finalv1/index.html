Instructions

Connect Four is a game played on a grid of 6 rows and 7 columns. In alternating turns players select a column to place their chip and the chip falls to the lowest available cell in the grid. Once a player has connected 4 chips vertically, horizontally, or diagonally they've won.

Etc
